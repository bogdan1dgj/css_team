Borsch

Ingredients

  • beets , potatoes , cabbage , onion , garlic
  • garlic , tomato paste , meat , water or broth
  • salt, pepper, bay leaf , vinegar or lemon juice
  • sugar , oil or butter , dill, parsley, sour cream

Instructions

  1. Place the meat in a pot, add 2 liters of water, bring to a boil, and simmer for 30–40 minutes, skimming off any foam.
  2. Grate the beets and sauté them with tomato paste for 5–7 minutes.
  3. Chop the carrot and onion and sauté them as well.
  4. Add potatoes to the broth; after 10 minutes, add cabbage, then the sautéed vegetables.
  5. Add bay leaves, salt, and pepper. Cook for another 10–15 minutes.
  6. Remove from heat and let the soup rest for 10–15 minutes under a lid.
